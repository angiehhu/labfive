Short paragraph about me.

My name's Angela Huang. You might hear some people call me Angie, Ange, G, or big sis. I was born and raised here in Metro Vancouver to an immigrant Chinese household. Like any story about being raised with immigrant parents, my first language isn't English; instead, it's Cantonese. I'd like to describe myself as a houseplant enthusiast, a proud member of the NUMTOT (new urbanist memes for transit-oriented teens) facebook group, and a musician. Drawing while listening to music is one of my favourite hobbies. I like listening to all types of music: from classical to trap rap. However, there is one particular genre of music that I really like to listen to, and that is Jazz music.
